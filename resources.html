Programs

Here is a list of resources to help support you in the tech field.

  • Girls Who Code, U.S based program that offers clubs open to 6th-12th and summer programs. The Summer Immersion Program introduces trans, cis girls, as well as non-binary kids, computer skills to help them prepare for a career in technology.
  • Black Girls Code, Their goal is to help African-American youth with the skills to be able to create and build by using technology. They hope to increase the number of women of color in the STEM field.
  • Code.org, a non-profit organization that is dedicated to expanding access to computer programming to minorities and girls. They have a freeone-hour tutorial designed program that introduces code.
  • Women in CyberSecurity,they're a professional organization to help increase the number of women in the tech field. They provide support, mentorship, training, networking opportunities, access to industry and academic leaders.
  • Girls in Tech, an organization to help women in technology. They host events, conferences, and workshops, along with coding boot camps and mentorship programs.
